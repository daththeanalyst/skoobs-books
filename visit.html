Visit Us

We'd love to see you in the Guildhall Market

How to Find Us

Address Stalls 40-42, Bath Guildhall Market, High Street, Bath BA2 4AW
Location Inside Bath's covered Guildhall Market. We're towards the middle of the market — just follow the spines.
Phone 01225 463133

Opening Hours

Mon–Sat 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Sunday Closed

Getting Here

By Train Bath Spa station is a 10-minute walk. Head up Manvers Street towards the city centre and the Abbey.
By Bus Most city buses stop at Bath Bus Station or Dorchester Street, both 5 minutes' walk from the Market.
By Car Nearest car parks: Charlotte Street (3 min walk), Southgate (5 min). Bath city centre is very walkable.

While You're Here

The Guildhall Market is home to around 20 independent stalls. After browsing our books, why not explore...

🍪

Nibbles Cheese

Over 90 varieties of cheese from around the world. A Guildhall Market institution for 40+ years.

☕

Gillard's of Bath

Tea and coffee merchants since 1886. Grab a cup and browse your new book.

🏭

Bath Abbey

Just 80 yards from the market entrance. One of England's most beautiful churches.

Skoobs Books

Bath's favourite second-hand bookshop. Over 30 years in the heart of Guildhall Market.
