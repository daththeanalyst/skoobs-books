About Skoobs

Over 30 years of great books at great prices

Our Story

Bath's Favourite Bookshop

Skoobs Books — that's "BOOKS" spelled backwards — has been a fixture of Bath's Guildhall Market for over thirty years. Not to be confused with the London bookshop of the same name in Bloomsbury, we are wholly independent and very much a Bath original.

Our wooden market stall at Stalls 40-42 (opposite Sew 'n' Sew), neatly lined with thousands of alphabetically ordered titles, has become a regular stop for Bath locals and visitors alike. We pride ourselves on offering a well-curated selection of quality second-hand books — neatly organised, in excellent condition, and at prices that make reading accessible to everyone.

Our Philosophy

Good Books Deserve Good Homes

We believe every book deserves to be read more than once. When you buy a second-hand book from Skoobs, you're giving a story a new chapter — and keeping a good book in circulation rather than gathering dust.

Our stock is always changing. We carefully select every book we put out, ensuring quality condition and a genuine breadth of reading. From the latest bestsellers that arrive within weeks of publication, through to classic novels and hidden gems you won't find anywhere else.
